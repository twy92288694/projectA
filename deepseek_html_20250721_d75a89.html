Sharp Island (橋咀島)

Located in the Sai Kung Volcanic Rock Region, Sharp Island offers fascinating geological formations both above and below water. The island is famous for its tombolo that appears during low tide.

  • Depth: 5-15 meters
  • Visibility: 5-10 meters
  • Marine Life: Coral communities, sea urchins, starfish
  • Best Season: April to November

Bluff Island (甕缸群島)

Known for its dramatic sea cliffs and caves, Bluff Island offers spectacular wall dives with colorful soft corals and abundant marine life.

  • Depth: 10-25 meters
  • Visibility: 8-15 meters
  • Marine Life: Lionfish, moray eels, nudibranchs
  • Best Season: May to October

Ninepin Group (果洲群島)

This remote island group features spectacular hexagonal rock columns and clear waters, offering some of Hong Kong's best drift dives.

  • Depth: 8-20 meters
  • Visibility: 10-20 meters
  • Marine Life: Barracuda, batfish, occasional reef sharks
  • Best Season: June to September

Basalt Island (火石洲)

Named for its volcanic rock formations, Basalt Island offers dramatic underwater topography including caves, arches, and swim-throughs.

  • Depth: 5-30 meters
  • Visibility: 5-15 meters
  • Marine Life: Groupers, octopus, cuttlefish
  • Best Season: April to December

Information referenced from Hong Kong Tourism Board
